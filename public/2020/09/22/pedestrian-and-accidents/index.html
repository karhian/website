Pedestrian and accidents

2020-09-22

Introduction

I have obtained a dataset from Open Data Toronto (Gelfand (2020)). This dataset consist of transporation data from 140 neighbourhoods in Toronto for year 2008 and 2011.

This bar chart shows that Yorkdale-Glen Park has the most number of pedestrian collision incident for both years.
